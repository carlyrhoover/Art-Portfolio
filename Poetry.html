Divine Wiring

New programming and systems analysis.
Voltage, current, diesel generators and Trojan horse programs.
Security, technology, and human nature.
A human operator? Give blood to me.
The physical and cyber world
A fence 8 feet high topped with concertina wire signals.
A greater need to keep intruders out.
And sometimes, to keep people in.
Detection of an intruder.
Subversion error is fooling the system over time.
The security of these mechanisms, a chain is only as good as its weakest link.
Cyberterrorism, cyberweapons, and cyberwars.
This piece of malware was designed to sabotage plants, to damage industrial systems.
Things even more insidious than that.

O King of Kings!

O King of Kings! Your dastardly plan has come to fruition.
Mutually assured destruction kept at bay.
No warheads. No fallout.
Your Majesty!
Your Majesty!
Your Majesty! You’ve won, but at what cost?
Rise, and see the city you’ve level’d.
Three million lambs, sent to slaughter.
Your scale tip’d, three million exchanged for seven billion.
O King of Kings! Have you no heart?
Is your head filled with only thoughts of yourself?
Your Majesty does not answer, no answer am I worthy.
No answer do I deserve.
(In reference to the Watchmen character, Ozymandius, who averted nuclear warfare by attacking New York City, effectively uniting the world)
